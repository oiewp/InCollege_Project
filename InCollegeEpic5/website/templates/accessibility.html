Accessibility

InCollege is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards to ensure we provide equal access to all users.

Measures to Support Accessibility

InCollege takes the following measures to ensure accessibility:

  • Incorporation of accessibility into our development processes.
  • Continuous training of our staff on accessibility best practices.
  • Clear accessibility targets and responsibilities.
  • Regular testing for accessibility on our platform.

Conformance Status

We are committed to following the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) and ensuring our website conforms to the AA standard.

Compatibility with Browsers and Assistive Technology

InCollege is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • Browser X with assistive technology Y on operating system Z.
  • Browser A with assistive technology B on operating system C.

Technical Specifications

Accessibility of InCollege relies on the following technologies to work with the particular combination of web browser and any assistive technologies or plugins installed on your computer:

  • HTML
  • WAI-ARIA
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

Limitations and Alternatives

Despite our best efforts to ensure accessibility of InCollege, there may be some limitations. Please contact us if you observe an issue.

Assessment Approach

InCollege assessed the accessibility by the following approaches:

  • Self-evaluation
  • External evaluation
